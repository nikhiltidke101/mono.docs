## articles-count

Get count of articles matching given filter.

### Query Parameters

<Property required={false} deprecated={false} name={"ancestor"} type={"string"}>

The ancestor directory of the articles.

</Property>

<Property required={false} deprecated={false} name={"applies_to_parts"} type={"array of string"}>

Filters for articles belonging to any of the provided parts.


<span>Example:  `["PROD-12345"]`</span>

</Property>

<Property required={false} deprecated={false} name={"authored_by"} type={"array of string"}>

Filters for articles authored by any of the provided users.


<span>Example:  `["DEVU-12345"]`</span>

</Property>

<Property required={false} deprecated={false} name={"created_by"} type={"array of string"}>

Filters for articles created by any of the provided users.

<span>Example:  `["DEVU-12345"]`</span>

</Property>

<Property required={false} deprecated={false} name={"modified_by"} type={"array of string"}>

Filters for articles modified by any of the provided users.


<span>Example:  `["DEVU-12345"]`</span>

</Property>

<Property required={false} deprecated={false} name={"owned_by"} type={"array of string"}>

Filters for articles owned by any of the provided users.

<span>Example:  `["DEVU-12345"]`</span>

</Property>

## create-article

Article is an object which can contain a URL or artifacts in the
resource. It also contains the data regarding the owner, author, status
and published date of the object. This call creates an article.

The authors of the article.

<span>Example:  `["DEVU-12345"]`</span>

Description for the article.

</Property>

<span>Example:  `["ARTIFACT-12345"]`</span>

</Property>

<span>Example:  `["DEVU-12345"]`</span>
